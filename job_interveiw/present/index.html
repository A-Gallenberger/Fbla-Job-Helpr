Job Interveiw Presentation

Here you can find advice about how to present yourself;

  1. Reveiw your past; Where have you worked before, what postitions did you hold, how long were you in those jobs for, what did you learn from those jobs?
  2. Assess your present; What is your current job, what are your current responsibilities, how would improve yourself right now?
  3. Envision your future; What are your goals, do you plane to pursue more education, would you like to achieve a higher leadership position?
  4. Know who you are; In addition to steps 1-3, know what you are good at and where you could stand to use improvement.
  5. Practice what you might say if they ask you, "What is your greatest strength?" or "Why do you want this job?"
  6. Dressing Approperatly; Dress for the job you want, so a suit for business or teaching. Make sure the suit is clean and is free of hairs or fuzz.
