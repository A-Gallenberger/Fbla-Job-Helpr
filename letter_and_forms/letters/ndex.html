Here is the correct way to fill out cover letters and resumes;

  • Cover Letter, you should include;
    1.Name, address, phone number and email.
    2.Date of when you write it.
    3.Name and address of the company you are writing to.
    4.Dear (the name of the employer),
    5.Write of how you found the job opertunity, say what you have all sent with this letter, say why you are interested and what you have all acheieved.
    6.Say, " i can be reached at (your email) or at my cell at (your number).
    7.Thank them for their time and consideration.
    8.Sincerly,
    Your signature (for hard copies)
    Your name

  • Resume, you should include;
    1.Name, address, phone number, and email.
    2.Qualifications you have; like if you have a background in what you are applying for, how your work ethic is, and how well you know technology as in; telephones, computers, etc.
    3.Professional Experiences; What you have done in the past jobs.
    4.Past Job; Name, Location, and years there; will help to show that you have had past experiences working.
    5.Education; High School, College, other; include what years you were in school and if you had honors or high honors.
    6.Achievements and Accolades; Shows what you have all done in your lifeitme and to show that you are a hardworker.
