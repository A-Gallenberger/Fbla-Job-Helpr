Here is the correct way to fill out a application;

  1. Be prepared; Know the correct names, dates, places, and other information you'll need for your application.
  2. Read the form; Make sure to use pen when says "use pen only," or whenyou have to list information in a certain order.
  3. Be neat; Use a pen, or type the information in the application if you can take the form home. If you mkae a mistake, use whiteout to fix your mistake.
  4. Answer all questions, and make sure the answers are correct.
  5. Be positive; List what you have done, and haven't done. An example is, if you have been unemployed for a period of time, show the work you have been busy with, such as going to school, managing a household or doing carpentry on your home.
  6. Be clear; Know what position you're after and the right income range.
  7. References; Make sure you know if your references know you are using them on your application.
  8. Re-read your application; Make sure everything is spelt rights, and all the right dates are correct. Once done, you mail it in or hand it in. PS, make sure to send a cover letter re-stating your interests and qualifications.
